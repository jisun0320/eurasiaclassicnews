About

Eurasia Classic News is a bilingual classical music publication connecting stories, artists, and audiences across Europe and Asia.

We cover the performers, ensembles, festivals, venues, and collaborations shaping contemporary concert life, with a particular focus on the exchange between Europe and Korea.

Through reporting, interviews, and editorial features in English and Korean, ECN brings classical music stories beyond their place of origin and into a wider international conversation.
